## Three Layers — Each One Saves More Time

### Layer 1: The Prompt Library (Start Here)

78 workflows your team can use immediately. Copy, paste into Claude or Perplexity, go. Each one includes:
- Which AI tool to use and why
- What files to upload (briefs, data, brand guidelines)
- A detailed instruction set that gets quality output first time — not the "write me a media plan" nonsense that produces generic slop

**This is the fastest win.** Your team is using it on day one.

### Layer 2: Installed Skills (The Consistency Layer)

33 skills that install directly into Claude. Once installed, they teach the AI a repeatable process. Instead of explaining what you want every time, your strategist just says: *"Use the Competitor Analysis skill. Here's the brand."*

Every person on the team gets the same quality output. The junior planner's competitor analysis follows the same framework as the Head of Strategy's. That's how you scale quality without scaling headcount.

### Layer 3: Desktop Automation (The Speed Layer)

12 plugin bundles with slash commands. Your team types `/status-report` or `/client-health` and the AI builds the document on their computer. No copy-pasting between windows. No reformatting.

This is where the time savings compound. A weekly status report that takes 45 minutes becomes a 3-minute command.

### 5. Protect client confidentiality

This deploys on your infrastructure. Client data, media plans, and competitive intelligence never touch a third-party SaaS platform. For clients in sensitive categories — financial services, healthcare, government — that matters.
